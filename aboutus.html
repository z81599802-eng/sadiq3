Our mission

Building a real-time command center for ecommerce teams

We started SellersSpark after years of managing high-growth ecommerce accounts and watching teams drown in spreadsheets. Our mission is simple: give sellers the clarity to reinvest their energy into building loved products.

Built from operator pain

Every workflow is designed to cut through reconciliation chaos, give instant visibility, and prevent revenue leakage.

Automation-first

From payouts to inventory syncs, we automate the tedious so teams can prioritise growth experiments and launches.

Confidence with context

Insights, alerts, and recommendations always come with transparent sources so leaders can act decisively.

What guides us

Principles that shape our products and support

Transparency

No black boxes. Every reconciliation, insight, and alert can be traced back to a documented source.

Partnership

We win only when our customers grow. Dedicated success managers co-own targets and share operating best practices.

Reliability

Mission-critical operations need dependable tooling. We design for uptime, accuracy, and secure data handling.

Leadership & team

Operators, analysts, and engineers aligned around growth

SellersSpark is led by operators, analysts, and engineers who have scaled D2C labels and marketplace-first brands. We are headquartered in Bengaluru with remote teammates across India.
